About me

Coder, Researcher, Writer, Singer

I was working as an assistant professor of Classics in Winter 2020 when the pandemic hit. As I moved my courses online, I became passionate about the power of front-end development to deliver quality educational experiences.

I enrolled in SheCodes React in Fall 2020 and will begin my journey as a React developer in May 2021 with a local instructional design company.
